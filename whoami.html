Hi everyone, I'm John Doe and that's my story: Born in Mantua in 1988, I relocated to New York to pursue architecture studies at Columbia University, where I graduated in Architectural Design in 2015. From the early years of university, I became interested in photography, a passion I cultivated alongside art and design. These overlapping interests fueled my creative pursuits throughout my studies. Upon returning to my hometown, I began collaborating with a design studio and embarked on a career as a photographer. I initially discovered photography as a tool for understanding my surroundings. My personal projects are interpretations of urban contexts in both Italy and abroad, aiming to find a formal synthesis of the city through its objects: Understanding and Expression. My skills acquired during my architectural studies and technical knowledge gained through my passion form the foundation of my professional work for design studios and private clients.
